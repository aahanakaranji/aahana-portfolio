About Me

I am a junior at Etowah High School, currently ranked 6th out of 490 students, with a 4.1 GPA and 102.56 GPA. I am passionate about both technology and the arts.

I have been taking piano lessons for 9 years, and I volunteer as an ambassador with the Smile Up organization to give back to my community. This summer, I am offering free piano lessons to help others experience the joy of music.

Academically, I have pursued AP Computer Science for two years with top scores and am an active member of the Mu Alpha Theta (Math Honor Society) and National English Honor Society. I lead my school’s Model UN club, where I develop skills in public speaking, leadership, and global awareness, and I have tutored my peers through my school's tutoring program in a variety of subjects.

Outside of school, I am a history enthusiast and a fan of anime. With my multicultural upbringing, I bring a unique perspective and drive to my work and learning.

I am applying to top computer science programs to further my studies and contribute to a global community of learners and innovators.

I believe in working hard and helping others. This website is a way for me to share what I’ve learned and offer something back (free piano lessons). I’m grateful for the opportunities I’ve had, and excited to continue learning.
