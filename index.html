About Me

I am a junior at Etowah High School, currently ranked 6th out of 490 students, with a 4.5 GPA and 102.56 GPA. I am passionate about both technology and the arts.

I have been taking piano lessons for 9 years, and I volunteer as an ambassador with the Smile Up organization to give back to my community. This summer, I am offering free piano lessons to help others experience the joy of music.

Academically, I have pursued AP Computer Science for two years with top scores and am an active member of the Math Honors Society and English Language Honors Society. I also lead my school’s Model UN club, where I develop skills in public speaking, leadership, and global awareness.

Outside of school, I am a history enthusiast and a fan of anime. As an immigrant, I bring a unique perspective and drive to my work and learning.

I am applying to top computer science programs to further my studies and contribute to a global community of learners and innovators.
