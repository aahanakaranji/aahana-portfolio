About Me

Hi! My name is Aahana Karanji!

I am a 17-year-old who has been playing piano since I was 9. I like to read and listen to music. I am studying to be a software programmer, in fact, I made this website! I am a junior at Etowah High School, and I live in Eagle Watch.

I love playing piano with all my heart! Piano has been a huge part of my life. Even indirectly, piano has helped me with other areas like math, language skills, my attention and memory, and improved my fine motor skills, like typing faster!

My piano journey started in the third grade. I had always loved playing on my old keyboard, so I wanted to learn officially. I started my lessons, and I loved them! I still remember the first song I ever learnt, Hot Cross Buns. I remember my first piano recital, it was terrifying but I loved it. However, my songs were short and took me less than 30 seconds, so that probably helped my first ever experience.

Other than piano, I love to watch movies with my mother, read books, learn about history, and listen to a variety of genres of music. Growing up, I loved Harry Potter and Percy Jackson, but now my reading had reduced, because I spend more time programming and studying physics.
